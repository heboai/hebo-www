Frequently Asked Questions

Which LLM models does Hebo support? +

Hebo supports a wide range of LLM models including OpenAI's GPT-3.5, GPT-4, Anthropic's Claude, and open-source models like Llama 2. Our platform is designed to be model-agnostic, allowing you to easily switch between different providers or use your own custom models.

Does Hebo integrate with my existing systems? +

Yes, Hebo is designed to integrate seamlessly with your existing systems. We provide APIs, webhooks, and SDKs for popular programming languages to make integration straightforward. Our platform can connect with your CRM, ticketing systems, knowledge bases, and other tools you already use.

How is my data in Hebo secured? +

Data security is our top priority. Hebo uses industry-standard encryption for data at rest and in transit. We are SOC 2 Type II compliant and regularly undergo security audits. Your data is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent, and you have full control over your data retention policies.

Can Hebo be deployed on premise? +

Yes, Hebo offers an on-premise deployment option for enterprises with strict data governance requirements. Our on-premise solution includes all the features of our cloud platform but runs within your own infrastructure, giving you complete control over your data and ensuring compliance with internal policies.

Is Hebo free to use? +

Hebo offers a free tier that allows you to get started with basic features and a limited number of API calls. For teams and businesses, we offer tiered pricing plans based on usage and feature requirements. Contact our sales team for a custom quote tailored to your specific needs.
